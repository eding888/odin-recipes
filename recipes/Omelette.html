Omelette

Omelettes are the product of cracking unborn chicken babies into a pan until they solidify into a yellow semicircular block. Yummers. They are a notable breakfast food.

Let's find out how this delicious egg-based dish is made.

Ingredients:
  • Eggs (chicken kind)
  • Cheese
  • Bell peppers of various hues
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl. Any old bowl will do.
  2. Beat the eggs until it becomes a slimy yellow paste. Yummerz
  3. Add some butter to a skillet, put said skillet on stove, and turn on said stove to medium heat.
  4. Chop up bell peppers into little cubes.
  5. Add yellow egg paste into pan. Add your little cubes of bell pepper as well.
  6. Do not be jostling up those eggs!! Let it slowly cook into a sheet. Add some cheese into the middle of the egg sheet and then fold it.
