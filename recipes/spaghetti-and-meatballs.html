Spaghetti and Meatballs

Spaghetti and Meatballs is a recipe that originates from Italy (or so we're told). It is theorized that this dish originates from an otherworldy planet like Mars, but I digress.

Let's find out how to make this delicious noodle dish.

  • Ingredients:

    •
  • A good bit of spaghetti (depending on how hungry you are)
  • A jar of your favorite Italian tomato sauce
  • Some of those pre-cooked meatballs from Costco (who has the time to make meatballs from scratch..)
  • Cheese of the parmesean variety
